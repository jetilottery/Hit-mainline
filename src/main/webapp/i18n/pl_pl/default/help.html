HIT

Time to break the piggy bank in HIT! Have a smashing good time matching numbers and revealing multi-pliers. You might even find the Win All piggy bank! Collect hammers to enter the Bonus game for more chances of truly smashing wins!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

Match any Your Number to any Lucky Number and win a prize.

Reveal a 2x Multiplier symbol to double the prize amount.

Find a Win All symbol and win all prizes instantly.

Find 5 Hammer symbols in Your Numbers to enter the bonus game.

In the bonus, smash 3 coin filled piggy banks to win a prize. Smash 3 empty piggy banks, or win the top prize, to finish the game.

The game ends when all the Lucky Numbers, Your Numbers, and the Bonus ticket are revealed. A closing message appears, indicating the game’s outcome.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to play the game to completion automatically. Gameplay continues until all symbols are revealed.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Instant wins are added to total winning prize values.

Wins display as currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
